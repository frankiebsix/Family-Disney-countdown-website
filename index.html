EPCOT Flower & Garden Food

Festival food plan for your EPCOT days.

What this page is for

The 2026 EPCOT International Flower & Garden Festival runs March 4 through June 1, so it should be active during your May trip. This page gives a practical booth-by-booth shortlist: two items to consider and one gluten-free-friendly pick where the research supports it. The official Disney photos are linked instead of embedded to avoid copying Disney-owned images.

Allergy reminder: Disney says guests should speak with a Cast Member about allergy-friendly requests, cross-contact cannot be guaranteed, and menus can change. Use this as a planning list, then verify at the booth.

Quick hit list

  • Best savory route: Northern Bloom scallops or beef tips, Trowel & Trellis pork ribs, Magnolia Terrace shrimp po’ boy.
  • Best gluten-free-friendly route: Northern Bloom, La Isla Fresca, Beach Grub corn, Yacht Grub dips/chips, Pineapple Promenade DOLE Whip in a cup.
  • Kid-friendly / easy wins: Frozen Desert Violet Lemonade, Orange-Lemon Smoothie, Frushi, DOLE Whip Pineapple or Peach.
  • Important date note: Florida Fresh is listed through May 25; EPCOT visitors on May 27 likely should not count on that booth.
CommuniCore Plaza • Available through May 25
