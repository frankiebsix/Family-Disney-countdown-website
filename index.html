EPCOT Flower & Garden Food

Festival food plan for your EPCOT days.

What this page is for

The 2026 EPCOT International Flower & Garden Festival runs March 4 through June 1, so it should be active during your May trip. This page gives a practical booth-by-booth shortlist and restores the official Disney photo/menu link for every booth.

Allergy reminder: speak with a Cast Member about allergy-friendly requests. Cross-contact cannot be guaranteed and menus can change.


Sources used

Based on 2026 menu data from Disney Parks Blog, Undercover Tourist, Disney Food Blog, WDW News Today, Disney’s official allergy-friendly page, and Gluten Free & Dairy Free at WDW.
